# Privacy Policy

Scorpius Capital Group LLC (the "Company") is a private proprietary trading institution. The Company operates exclusively as a self-funded enterprise, trading financial instruments solely on its own account using proprietary capital. The Company does not solicit, accept, or manage third-party funds, nor does it offer any brokerage, advisory, or financial services to external clients or the general public.

Since the Company does not provide public services or manage external client accounts, this website does not collect, process, or store any personal data or user-identifying information.
